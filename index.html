Privacy Policy – SwipeSort Video

Last updated: 2026-03-27

```

SwipeSort Video respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles data, which permissions it uses, and how third-party services may process certain information.

1. General

SwipeSort Video is a mobile application that allows users to sort videos locally on their device using swipe gestures.

The app does not require user accounts and does not operate a custom backend for storing your personal files. Your videos remain on your device unless you explicitly delete them yourself through your device’s media system.

2. Data Collection and Processing

a) Local Media Processing

The app requests access to your device’s media library in order to display and sort videos. This access is only used for the core functionality of the app.

  • The app accesses videos stored on your device only with your permission.
  • Sorting actions such as keep, review, or delete list handling are processed locally.
  • Your videos are not uploaded to our own servers.
  • The app is designed to work primarily on-device.

b) Analytics (Firebase)

SwipeSort Video may use Google Firebase Analytics to better understand app usage and improve the product over time.

Analytics data may include:

  • App usage events
  • Session activity
  • Screen views
  • Basic device and app information
  • Interaction behavior inside the app

This information is used in aggregated or anonymous form and is not intended to directly identify you. Where required, analytics collection is only enabled after your consent.

c) Advertising (AdMob)

SwipeSort Video may display ads using Google AdMob.

Ad-related processing may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • Ad impressions
  • Ad interaction data
  • Consent status

Depending on your region and your consent choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect and manage consent where applicable.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob

These providers may process data according to their own privacy terms and policies.

4. Permissions

SwipeSort Video may request the following permissions:

  • Media library / video access – required to display and sort videos on your device

5. Data Retention

SwipeSort Video does not maintain its own remote database for storing your personal videos. Any local sorting data or app state may remain on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or remove content yourself.

Analytics and advertising-related data may be processed and retained by Google according to Google’s own retention policies.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, including countries in the EEA, UK, or similar regions, you may have certain privacy rights.

  • You may accept or decline consent where legally required.
  • You may disable analytics through app settings where available.
  • You can revoke permissions through your device settings.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce unnecessary data exposure. The app is designed so that core video sorting functionality happens locally on your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

SwipeSort Video is not directed specifically to children. The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact:

Email: your@email.com
```
