# Privacy Policy – SwipeSort Video

Last updated: 2026-03-27

SwipeSort Video respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles data.

## 1. General

SwipeSort Video is a mobile application that allows users to sort videos locally on their device using swipe gestures.

We do not require user accounts and we do not store personal data on external servers.

## 2. Data Collection

### a) Local Data Processing

* The app accesses your device’s media library (videos only) with your permission.
* All processing happens locally on your device.
* No videos are uploaded.

### b) Analytics (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics.

Collected:

* App usage
* Device type
* Interaction behavior

This data is anonymous and not used to identify you.

### c) Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect:

* Device identifiers
* Ad interaction data

We use Google UMP for consent.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell data.

Used services:

* Google Firebase
* Google AdMob

## 4. Permissions

* Media library access (videos)

## 5. Security

All processing is local.
